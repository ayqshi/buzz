PRIVACY POLICY
Last updated: January 1, 2025

1. Overview

Buzz ("we", "the app") is a client-side application. The vast majority of your data never leaves your device. This policy explains what data we handle and how.

2. Data We Store Locally (Your Browser)

Account credentials: Your email and encoded password are stored in your browser's localStorage. Your password is never transmitted to any server.

User profile: Your name, company, specialty, and preferences are stored locally. This information is used only to personalize email templates within the app.

API keys: Any Claude API key you enter is stored in localStorage on your device only. It is used exclusively to make direct API calls from your browser to Anthropic's servers. Buzz does not receive or log your API key.

Lead data: Business lists, contacted/unchecked statuses, and notes are stored locally in your browser.

3. Data Transmitted to Third Parties

OpenStreetMap / Overpass API: When you search, your location query and approximate search area are sent to the Overpass API to retrieve business data. This is necessary for the core functionality of the app. OpenStreetMap's privacy policy applies.

Nominatim / Photon: Location queries are sent to geocoding services to resolve place names to coordinates.

Anthropic API: If you use AI email generation, business context (name, type, location) is sent directly from your browser to Anthropic's API. Your API key authenticates this request. Anthropic's privacy policy applies to this data.

No other data sharing: We do not sell, share, or transmit your personal information to any other third party.

4. Cookies & Tracking

Buzz does not use cookies. Buzz does not contain any analytics, tracking pixels, or advertising scripts.

5. Data Retention

Your data persists in your browser's localStorage until you clear it or use the "Delete My Data" option in Settings. Logging out does not delete your stored data — it only ends your session.

6. Your Rights

You can delete all locally stored data at any time from Settings → "Delete All My Data". You can also clear your browser's localStorage directly. You have the right to access and delete any data stored about you.

7. Children's Privacy

Buzz is not intended for use by anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Significant changes will be noted in the app.

9. Contact

Questions about this Privacy Policy? Use the feedback option within the app.
